How to Play

Defeat 100 waves of critters!

STATS

  • HP (Health): How much damage you can take before losing.
  • ATK (Attack): How much damage your attacks deal.
  • HYPE (Bonus): Temporarily increases attack power.

ACTIONS

  • Attack: Deal damage. Foe attacks back.
  • Heal: Restore HP. Foe attacks back.
  • Dance: HYPE may increase for you, the foe, or both.
  • Run: Escape the current foe, regain a little HP, lose HYPE, and face a new foe.
